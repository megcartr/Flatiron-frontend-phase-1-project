LET'S MEDITATE!
Meditation is an important exercise in which benefits your physical, mental, and spiritual well being. Start the timer below and close you eyes. Focus on inhaling and exhaling equally. For example, inhale for 4 seconds and exhale for 4 seconds. As you are breathing, focus on clearing your mind. It will be difficult at first, but with practice it become more natural. Let's begin!
